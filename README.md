# AI Bubble Research & Stochastic Equity Simulations

Research project investigating whether AI-related equities exhibit bubble-like dynamics and whether classical financial models such as Black–Scholes adequately capture the behavior of modern AI-driven markets.

---

# Overview


---

# Main Research Questions

* Do AI-related equities exhibit speculative bubble behavior?
* Are Black–Scholes assumptions realistic for AI stocks?
* How do stochastic volatility and jump models compare to constant-volatility models?
* Do AI equities exhibit heavy tails or volatility clustering?

---

# Models Considered

## Black–Scholes Model

Baseline diffusion process:

[
dS_t = \mu S_t dt + \sigma S_t dW_t
]

Assumptions:

* Constant volatility
* Lognormal returns
* Continuous price paths
